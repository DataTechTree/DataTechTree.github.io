  • Unit Vector ∥x∥2=1\Vert \mathbf{x} \Vert_2=1∥x∥2​=1

    • we usually look for unit eigenvectors.
  • Suppose that a matrix A\mathbf{A}A has nnn linearly independent eigenvectors $v{(1)},...,v{(n)} $ with corresponding eigenvalues λ1,...,λn\lambda_1, ..., \lambda_nλ1​,...,λn​. The eigendecomposition of A\mathbf{A}A is given by A=Vdiag(λ)V−1\mathbf{A}=\mathbf{V} diag(\lambda) \mathbf{V}^{-1}A=Vdiag(λ)V−1
